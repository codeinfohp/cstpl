Leadership

Ashwani Acharya, Business Head of CSTPL is a qualified Management graduate in Business Administration and holds an Aeronautical Engineering degree from the Aeronautical Society of India. He is also an enormously experienced and competent Aircraft Maintenance Engineer with over 28 years of experience in aviation industry and AMEL license/type ratings on aircraft ranging from Cessna 152 to Boeing 777 and Airbus A319/320/321. He also holds a EASA B1 and Indian AME license from the Indian Director General of Civil Aviation.

His expertise includes Technical management & administration with broad exposure in financial and economic models. He has been leading the CSTPL team successfully since Aug 2014 and added new dimensions and vision to the team as a Business leader and core member of the Executive leadership.
